💌 From the heart

Dear Elizabeth

Some people arrive quietly, then become impossible to forget.

Betty, my Liz Favee, my Bby, I may not always have the perfect words, but I know what my heart has been saying for years. You became more than a girl I liked. You became home, peace, trouble, laughter, prayer, and the one name my heart keeps running back to.

We first crossed paths in 2019. We were not close then, but maybe life was already saving a place for us. In August 2021, friendship started to grow. Then October came, and your affection became louder in the sweetest way. You visited often, complimented me when I needed it most, and made me feel seen at a time when that meant everything.

On October 21, 2021, around 6am while I was going to school, I finally told you I loved you. It was not just a random feeling. It had been building through your visits, your words, your care, and the way you looked out for me.

Our story has not been perfect. We have had distance, silence, pain, lessons, forgiveness, and moments that tested us. But even after everything, I still see you with love. I changed. I stopped being just a boy. I learned that mistakes should teach us, not become weapons we keep using against each other.

You are Elizabeth. You are Betty. You are Liz Favee. You are shy sometimes, you laugh a lot, and sometimes you are boring in the cutest way, so I cheer you up. To me, you are still the person I can open up to, explain my future to, and trust with the secrets I do not give everyone.

I still love you. You have always been my person.
